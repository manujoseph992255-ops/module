8. Power Query & Pivot

Handling "Big Data" with Scalable Infrastructure

The Strategic Logic

Professionals never "clean" the same data twice. If your data exceeds 100,000+ rows, standard formulas become slow and volatile. Use Business Intelligence tools to process millions of rows without crashing Excel.

1. Power Query (ETL): Automated Cleaning

Power Query is the Extract, Transform, and Load (ETL) engine of Excel. It records your cleaning steps and repeats them with one click.

  • Business Case: You receive 12 monthly CSV files from multiple boutique branches. Instead of manual copy-pasting, Power Query merges and cleans all 12 files into a single master table in seconds.

2. Power Pivot (Data Modeling)

Think of Power Pivot as a mini SQL database inside your Excel. It links Sales, HR, and Finance tables via Relationships instead of VLOOKUPS.

  • Scalability: Modeling allows you to calculate complex metrics across tables, such as "Revenue per Rupee of Salary" across the entire company, without writing a single formula in the cell sheet.
