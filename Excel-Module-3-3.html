3.3 Finance Budget vs Actual Analysis

The Financial Compass

A static budget is useless. A dynamic Budget vs. Actual (BvA) model allows the enterprise to pivot when raw material costs (like silk or organic cotton) fluctuate. It measures the "Variance" and identifies the "Who, What, and Why" of financial drift.

= (Actual_Cost - Budget_Cost) / Budget_Cost
// Variance % calculation with dynamic range referencing

Mastering Variance Analysis

  • Conditional Triggering: Automatically highlighting variances greater than 10% in Deep Red.
  • Waterfall Logic: Designing sheets that show the step-by-step impact of volume vs. price on the final profit.
  • Dynamic Time-Framing: Using a single toggle to switch the entire report from "Monthly" to "Year-to-Date."
The "Audit Trail" Rule

In BvA modeling, every "Actual" cell must be traceable back to its original invoice or general ledger export. Never "Hard-Plug" an actual value into a reporting summary.
